Submitting Your Hack üìÆ

hackkean2017.devpost.com


Follow these steps:

1. Have all your team members create individual Devpost accounts.

2. Submit your project through the HackKean Devpost (making sure to add everyone on your team to your project).

3. Choose your prize categories.


All submissions are due on Sunday, April 2 at 11am.


After you finish working on your project on Sunday morning, you will have 30 minutes to prepare a short 3-minute presentation. During presentations, every team will have the chance to share what they have been working on. After all of the teams have presented, the judges will convene to decide on winners.
